World Generation

Entirely new world generation, I'm scrapping the data packs and coding the world generation myself with the help of the Terra mod/plugin. the mod can be found at https://modrinth.com/plugin/terra.

I decided to use Terra as it works better on papermc than the data packs do and it's easier to setup up, run and update than the data packs. it does mean I have to start from scratch when it comes to the world generation and that it'll take even longer to make, but it'll be worth it in the long run.

With this, I'll be able to properly rework all the dimensions world generation not just the overworld. also, it'll allow me to properly test the custom terrain as well as I can customise everything I want, I'm not limited by the vanilla system. which means I can make some genuinely crazy terrain appear.

why did I decide to follow this route? well, it was either this or use a cobbled-together mess I understand but don't know the intricacy of which would have led to slower world gen, a large number of bugs, etc. Instead, I can use a project that will allow me to understand everything I add and how it affects the world, it will also allow me to completely customise the server world gen outside of vanilla limits and for me to not go and use hacky laggy ways to add some feature I want the world gen to have.

To give you an idea of everything I want to add I'll provide a brief summary for now, with more to add later.

- Completely custom vanilla biomes, being all the terrain will be reworked to fit the biome better.
- New Custom Trees, this will allow you to find trees as small as vanilla's most minor but that can also be so large that it covers several chunks with its canopy.
- Custom Overhauled Vanilla Structures, this is because some of the vanilla structures haven't been updated in years. I want to spice them up and make them somewhat harder while also making them something players will want to explore more off, I am currently thinking of adding different biome variants of certain structures. while also adding more depth to them with archaeology

The Profile System & Website Revamp & Chat Integration

The Profile system is complex and not completely planned out yet, but the idea is to create a unique player id on the server side to link all your server-related accounts together. that would be your discord, MC, Dynmap, Plan and website account.

It will allow me and the future mod/admin team to better handle rule breakers and it'll also allow for better security when it comes to the accounts linked to the server. I'm looking into adding 2-factor authentication to the website rewrite, but to use that to guard the dynmap and plan on the website as those are also planned to be integrated into the new website.

The website itself will have a profile page where you can see your own profile and it'll include settings to hide your progress from others. when I say progress, I mean it will show your current health as of when you were last online, hunger, defense, inventory, end chest inventory and your backpack inventory. it'll also show all your details that Plan shows, outside of certain details like player ip's and locations. and most of this will also be added to a ingame profile page in the menu system.

I plan on allowing it to also be previewed in Discord chat by a bot command, hence why it's all connected.

Now the website will be greatly expanded upon in the future as I plan on integrating the dynmap and Plan sites into the website while also adding the new profile system, I will be adding forums to the website alongside a wiki that will have all the server documentation on it. the forums I'm still looking into if it is feasible and how many issues it would cause me to integrate the discord with it so that you can read the forums on both the website and here on the server. then I'm also hoping to completely integrate the chat channels between the server, discord, dynmap and the website. but dynmap chat might be removed in favour of just having the chat linked to the website.
